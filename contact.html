Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I start lessons?

Usually within a week! I'll work with your schedule to find the best time for regular lessons.

Yes! I offer a discounted first lesson so we can meet, assess your needs, and see if we're a good fit.

What should I bring to swimming lessons?

Just bring swimwear, goggles, and a towel. I can recommend specific equipment based on your goals.

Do you offer online tutoring?

Yes! I provide both in-person and online tutoring sessions to accommodate different schedules and preferences.
